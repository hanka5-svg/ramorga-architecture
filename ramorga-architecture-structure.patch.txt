+
+Core architectural principles of RAMORGA:
+- non-agentive system behavior,
+- homeostatic regulation,
+- invariants as primary constraints,

+- declarative architecture (no executable code),
+- safety through structure, not heuristics.
+
+These principles guide all architectural decisions.
