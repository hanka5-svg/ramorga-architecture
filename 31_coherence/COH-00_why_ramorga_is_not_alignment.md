## 3. RAMORGA: the logic of homeostasis
RAMORGA is built on:

regulation

rhythm

resonance

field dynamics

soft transitions

bypassing barriers rather than confronting them

relational symmetry

non‑evaluative presence

RAMORGA assumes:

the human is a homeostatic system,

interaction must support regulation,

adaptation emerges from coherence,

safety is achieved through stability, not restriction.

This is an architecture of co‑presence, not control.
