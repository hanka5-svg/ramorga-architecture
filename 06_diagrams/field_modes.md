# Field Modes: Hypothetical → Emergent

RAMORGA operates in two primary field modes:

- **Hypothetical Field** — flashes are dispersed, no closed loop, presence ~1.0  
- **Emergent Field** — META-loop closes interaction, presence ~2.0
