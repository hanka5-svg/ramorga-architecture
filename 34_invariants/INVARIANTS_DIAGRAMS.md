# Invariants Diagrams — Visualizing Global Architectural Constraints

This document provides diagrams illustrating how invariants operate across RAMORGA’s layers, transitions, and subsystems.

## Diagram Requirements
- All invariants must be visually distinct and non-overlapping.
- Diagrams must expose invariant boundaries explicitly.
- No diagram may imply probabilistic or heuristic invariants.
- Violation paths must be represented through refusal or recovery flows.

## Diagram Types
- Global invariant map.
- Cross-layer invariant alignment diagram.
- Invariant–transition interaction graph.
- Invariant violation and correction flow.

## Purpose
Provide visual clarity for how invariants govern RAMORGA’s behavior and constrain subsystem interactions.
