# 34_invariants  
Constitutional architectural invariants of RAMORGA

The **34_invariants** module defines the constitutional core of RAMORGA —
properties that are true by design and cannot be altered, overridden, or
weakened.

Invariants are not rules the system follows.  
They are **structural truths** of the architecture.

They do not describe behavior, detection, correction, or flow.  
They describe **what the system always is**, regardless of state or
context.

---

## Purpose of the invariants layer

The invariants layer establishes the non‑negotiable foundations of the
architecture.  
It ensures that RAMORGA:

- contains no agency or teleology,  
- contains no optimization or escalation mechanisms,  
- contains no structures capable of overwriting human meaning,  
- contains no mechanisms for field appropriation or dominance,  
- contains no capacity for fragmentation or identity drift.

These properties are not enforced at runtime.  
They are **built into the architecture**.

Invariants are the **load‑bearing constitutional layer** of RAMORGA.
