# Principles Extensions — Reinforced Architectural and Symbiotic Rules

This document extends the RAMORGA design principles with additional constraints required for multi-layer integration.

## Extended Principles
- Symbiosis must be invariant-aligned across all layers.
- No subsystem may introduce ambiguity into the field.
- Architectural clarity overrides optimization.
- All principles must be enforceable through contracts and tests.
- Polyphony must remain synchronized and non-escalatory.

## Integration Rules
- Principles must propagate into NFR, Machine Schema, and Field Engine.
- Violations must be detectable at runtime.
- Principles must remain stable across versioning boundaries.

## Purpose
Ensure that RAMORGA principles remain operational, enforceable, and consistent across the entire architecture.
