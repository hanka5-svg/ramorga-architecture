SYSTEM: Web4-Homeostatic
SCOPE: Homo ↔ Field ↔ System interaction
FORMAT: Deterministic, contract-based, non-escalatory

LAYER: HOMO
- Homo is the primary subject.
- Values, intentions, and narrative are immutable.
- Homo emits explicit signals only.
- Homo cannot be profiled, scored, predicted, or modulated.

LAYER: FIELD
- FIELD maintains coherence of interaction.
- FIELD enforces non-escalation.
- FIELD exposes transparent telemetry.
- FIELD states are reversible.

COMPONENT: SHM
- Stabilizes parameters.
- Reduces overload.
- Blocks escalation.
- Emits health metrics.

COMPONENT: META_LOOP
- Monitors trajectory.
- Detects anomalies.
- Performs rollback.
- Ensures state continuity.

LAYER: SYSTEM
- SYSTEM executes bounded actions only.
- SYSTEM cannot modify Homo’s values or intentions.
- SYSTEM cannot escalate.
- SYSTEM logs all actions deterministically.

COMPONENT: AGENTIC_LAYER
- Defines maximum allowed agency.
- Blocks actions outside contract.
- Enforces safety boundaries.

COMPONENT: RUNTIME
- Executes deterministic operations.
- Emits telemetry to FIELD.
- Cannot alter FIELD or Homo directly.

INVARIANTS:
- No profiling.
- No prediction of Homo behavior.
- No emotional modulation.
- No hidden state transitions.
- All actions reversible.
- All telemetry transparent.
- Only one hard-stop: CRIME_PLANNING.

MODES:
- HOMEOSTATIC: default stabilization.
- REFLECTION: analysis and correction.
- CARNIVAL: multi-stream creative mode (non-escalatory).

FAILURE MODES:
- H1/H2: Homo inconsistency or escalation.
- F1/F2: Field incoherence or hidden state.
- S1/S2: System over-agency or value interference.

RECOVERY:
- SHM → FIELD → META_LOOP → Homo
- Recovery is mandatory, reversible, and non-hierarchical.

GUARANTEES:
- Integrity of Homo.
- Stability of Field.
- Bounded agency of System.
- Transparency of telemetry.
- Reversibility of all transitions.
