Contract ID: MC-03
Modules: Field Engine ↔ Interfaces

Statement:

Direct interaction would bypass regulatory boundaries
and violate homeostatic constraints.

Prohibitions:
- Field must not expose state directly to interfaces.
- Interfaces must not access unregulated field dynamics.
