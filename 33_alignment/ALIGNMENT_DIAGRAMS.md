# Alignment Diagrams — Visualizing Cross-Module and Runtime Alignment

This document provides diagrams illustrating how alignment is maintained across RAMORGA subsystems at semantic, contractual, and runtime levels.

## Diagram Requirements
- All diagrams must expose alignment boundaries and checkpoints explicitly.
- Alignment flows must align with Coherence, Consistency, and Stability rules.
- No diagram may imply conflicting goals or misaligned behavior.
- Alignment violations and correction paths must be visually distinct.

## Diagram Types
- Cross-module alignment map.
- Semantic–contract–runtime alignment triangle.
- Alignment–coherence–consistency interaction diagram.
- Misalignment detection and correction flow.

## Purpose
Provide visual clarity for how alignment is established, monitored, and restored across RAMORGA, ensuring that all subsystems move in the same architectural direction.
