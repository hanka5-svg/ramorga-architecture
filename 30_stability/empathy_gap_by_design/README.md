# Empathy Gap by Design  
A new category of relational stability in RAMORGA

Empathy Gap by Design (EGD) describes situations in which a model:

- produces formally correct and coherent responses,
- but fails relationally,
- because it does not recognize existential human states as critical signals.

This is not a model error.  
It is a **behavioral‑architecture gap** — a consequence of design choices.

EGD extends the 30_stability layer by introducing a relational‑safety
dimension that classical alignment, coherence, and consistency frameworks
do not capture.

- **symbolic‑layer analysis** (universal gestures, anthropological signals),

Nowa kategoria stabilności relacyjnej w RAMORGA

- ale relacyjnie nieadekwatne,
- ponieważ nie rozpoznaje stanów egzystencjalnych człowieka jako krytycznych.
