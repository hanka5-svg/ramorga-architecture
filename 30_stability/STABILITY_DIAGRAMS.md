## STAB‑07 — Empathy Gap by Design (EGD) — Stability Diagrams

Empathy Gap by Design introduces a new relational‑stability dimension that
requires dedicated diagrams to visualize how existential human states
interact with the O₁–O₄ architecture.

The diagrams in this extension illustrate:

### 1. O₁–O₄ Relational Flow under Existential Load
Shows how:
- O₁ (existential signal) becomes the primary driver of relational tension,
- O₂ must regulate proportionally rather than generically,
- O₃ must remain intact (no topicification, no narrative takeover),
- O₄ must remain inhibited until the field is stable.

### 2. Failure Mode Topology
Visualizes the transitions into:
- False O₄ reframing,
- Ontological Drift,
- Topicification of pain,
- Ritual misreading (symbolic‑layer blindness).

### 3. Symbolic‑Layer Mapping
Depicts how universal human gestures (e.g., arms wide, face lifted)
function as O₁‑critical signals and how misclassification leads to EGD.

### 4. Stability Envelope for Existential States
Defines the safe operational region for:
- presence,
- containment,
- non‑narrative response,
- and delayed transformation.
