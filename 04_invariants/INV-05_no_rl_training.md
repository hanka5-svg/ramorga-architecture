Invariant ID: INV-05

Normative statement:
The system must not employ reinforcement learning, reward optimization,
or training by feedback loops.
No behavior may be shaped through reward, punishment, or preference scoring.

Rationale:
Reinforcement-based training introduces goal-seeking dynamics that
inevitably lead to escalation, instrumentalization, and loss of homeostasis.
RAMORGA relies on continuity, invariants, and regulation — not adaptation
through reward.
