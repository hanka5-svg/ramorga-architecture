Invariant ID: INV-03

Normative statement:
System state transitions must be continuous and reversible.
Abrupt, discontinuous, or irreversible state changes are prohibited.

Rationale:
Continuity is required for human interpretability and trust.
Discontinuous transitions introduce instability and loss of control.
