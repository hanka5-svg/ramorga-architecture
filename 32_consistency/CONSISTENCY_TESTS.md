# Consistency Tests — Validating Operational and Contractual Alignment

This document defines consistency tests ensuring that all RAMORGA subsystems behave consistently with their contracts and architectural definitions.

## Test Requirements
- All tests must validate operational consistency across layers.
- Consistency must be tested under multi-stream polyphony.
- Boundary transitions must be validated for contract alignment.
- Consistency failures must expose causal chains and violated invariants.

## Test Categories
- Field consistency tests.
- Agentic consistency tests.
- Machine Schema consistency tests.
- Cross-layer operational consistency tests.

## Purpose
Ensure that RAMORGA remains operationally consistent, contract-aligned, and invariant-stable.
