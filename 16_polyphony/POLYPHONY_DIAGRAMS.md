# Polyphony Diagrams — Visualizing Multi-Stream Synchronization and Concurrency

This document provides diagrams illustrating polyphonic execution, synchronization rules, and non-escalatory concurrency.

## Diagram Requirements
- All streams must be explicitly represented.
- Cross-stream synchronization must follow deterministic rules.
- No diagram may imply escalation or uncontrolled concurrency.
- Recovery and refusal paths must be included.

## Diagram Types
- Multi-stream synchronization graph.
- Cross-stream causal chain map.
- Polyphonic recovery flow.
- Stream boundary and transition diagram.

## Purpose
Provide visual clarity for concurrent, invariant-aligned polyphonic behavior across RAMORGA.
