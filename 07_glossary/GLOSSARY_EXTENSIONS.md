# Glossary Extensions — Additional Terms for Cross-Layer Consistency

This document extends the glossary with additional architectural terms required for consistent interpretation across RAMORGA.

## Extended Terms
- **Causal Chain** — A deterministic sequence of events leading to a system decision.
- **Invariant Boundary** — A non-negotiable architectural constraint enforced across layers.
- **Field Synchronization** — Alignment of subsystem states within the field engine.
- **Agentic Boundary State** — A machine-schema state representing agent-level constraints.
- **Polyphonic Stream** — A concurrent execution path synchronized with field invariants.

## Purpose
Provide additional terminology required for interpreting extended modules and ensuring semantic stability across the architecture.
